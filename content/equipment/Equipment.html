--- title: "Facilities" ---

The Otago Coring Repository has the following facilities and equipment:

  • Geotek MultiSensor Core Logger
  • iTrax XRF core scanner
  • Geotek Core Splitter
  • Multiple Refrigerated Containers for Core Storage
  • Bench space with access to natural light for visual core logging

Other facilities available for analysis of cores and core samples at the University of Otago include

  • The Otago Paleomagnetic Research Facility located in the Geology Department. This facility contains
    • Cryogenic Magnetometer located in a magnetic feild free room
    • MFK1 Kappabridge
    • Vibrating Sample Magnetometer
  • Laser Grain Size Analyser located in the Geography Department
